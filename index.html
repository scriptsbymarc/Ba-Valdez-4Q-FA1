Become A WFP Partner

The World Food Programme is the leading humanitarian organization globally that operates in 123 countries and territories. Endlessly working wit an advocacy of saving lives and changing lives, the WFP has always made sure to provide people with proper aid. Especially during famines, disasters, and crises that call for immediate action. Moreover, the WFP has continuously made efforts to prevent climate change, settle conflicts and end hunger throughout the world. Powered by its labor force of over 20,000 staff volunteers worldwide, the organization has tirelessly worked with governments and different sectors worldwide, and implemented and executed hundreds of operations across the globe.

With $9.6 billion worth of raised donations from individuals across the world in 2021, the WFP was able to assist and help over 128 million lives worldwide. The continued support, partnership and patronage coming from kind-hearted individuals like you will directly contribute to the cause and impact hundreds and thousands more lives across the globe, with the WFP continuously that all donations it receives will proceed to be used wisely and appropriately in ways that will contribute to positive change in our society.

THe WFP has also furthered its commitment towards serving the people. Being recently awarded the 2020 Nobel Peace Prize, the WFP looks forwards to advancing, creating and participating in more ways that will create sustainability within fostered communities. Moreover, the WFP ensures its pledge to serve the common good of the people, mankind and the world.

Your generosity comes a long way with WFP. Begin partaking in the worldwide fight to end hunger now.
